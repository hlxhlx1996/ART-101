BTS

BTS, also known as Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment.


On the forth year of their debut, BTS were listed by Forbes as the most retweeted artist on Twitter in March 2016. In 2017, BTS won a Billboard Music Award for Top Social Artist. In June 2017, Time magazine named the band one of the 25 most influential people on the internet.
